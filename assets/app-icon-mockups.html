Clawd Tank Main App Icon Concepts (Round 3)

The Notification Bubble

Emphasizes the app's core function: notifications. A sleek macOS 3D speech bubble containing our pixel companion.

The Big Face

A bold, minimalist approach. The entire app icon becomes Clawd's face, styled with a modern 3D gradient and big friendly eyes.

The Voxel Pet

Elevates the 2D pixel art into a 3D isometric "voxel" model. Sits on a pedestal like a digital desktop toy or collectable.
