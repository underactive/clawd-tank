# Animation Plans: Clawd "Working"

This document outlines concepts for animating the Clawd character while it is "working on something" (e.g., compiling, fetching data, computing, building). 

## 1. The Furious Typist (Active Coding/Processing)
* **Action:** Clawd is intensely focused, with both little arms moving rapidly up and down (simulating typing on an invisible or pixel-art keyboard). 
* **Body Mechanics:** The torso has a very fast, shallow jitter or vibration to show intense speed. 
* **Eyes:** Squinted slightly (half-height rectangles) and locked dead ahead. Occasionally darting left to right as if reading lines of code.
* **Effects:** Tiny pixel-art `1`s and `0`s (or just little square data packets) floating rapidly up from the "keyboard" area and fading out.

## 2. The Builder / Mechanic (Compiling or Fixing)
* **Action:** Clawd is rhythmically swinging a tiny pixel-art hammer or wrench down in front of it.
* **Body Mechanics:** A deliberate, heavy bouncing motion. The body stretches up as the arm raises, and squashes down hard when the hammer strikes.
* **Eyes:** Squeezed shut (dashes) on impact, then pop open wide on the upswing.
* **Effects:** A small burst of yellow/orange pixel "sparks" (similar to the celebration sparkles, but faster and shooting outward) every time the tool hits the ground.

## 3. The Deep Thinker (Loading or Searching)
* **Action:** Clawd is trying to figure a problem out. One arm is resting on the ground, the other is lifted, tapping its "chin" (the bottom corner of the torso).
* **Body Mechanics:** A very slow, rhythmic side-to-side sway.
* **Eyes:** Looking upwards and to the side, occasionally blinking slowly. 
* **Effects:** Two or three tiny pixel-art gears slowly rotating above Clawd's head, or a classic pixel hourglass flipping over on a loop.

## 4. The Heavy Pusher (Downloading or Moving Data)
* **Action:** Clawd is facing sideways (or leaning hard to one side from a front view), pushing with both arms against an invisible wall or a heavy pixel block.
* **Body Mechanics:** The body is diagonally skewed/leaning. The legs are doing a fast, scrambling "running in place" animation, occasionally slipping.
* **Eyes:** Straining—maybe one eye is slightly larger than the other, or angled to look like furrowed brows.
* **Effects:** A little blue pixel "sweat drop" flying off Clawd's head every few seconds.

## 5. The Conductor (Executing a Script / Organizing)
* **Action:** Clawd looks like a maestro orchestrating data. Both arms are waving in smooth, looping figure-eight patterns.
* **Body Mechanics:** A smooth, graceful bobbing motion, opposite to the frantic typing.
* **Eyes:** Closed happily (dashes) or blinking calmly, looking very much in control.
* **Effects:** A stream of little colored pixel squares flowing smoothly in an arc over Clawd's head, from one hand to the other.

## 6. The Detective / Debugger (Searching for Errors)
* **Action:** Clawd holds a giant pixel-art magnifying glass and slowly sweeps it back and forth across the ground.
* **Body Mechanics:** Hunched down low, taking slow, exaggerated, sneaky steps. 
* **Eyes:** One eye is squeezed shut, and the other is wide open, looking directly through the magnifying glass.
* **Effects:** A subtle "scanning" beam of light occasionally passing over the ground.

## 7. The Juggler (Multitasking / High Processing)
* **Action:** Clawd is frantically juggling three or four glowing data packets (little pixel squares) in a continuous loop.
* **Body Mechanics:** A rhythmic side-to-side rocking motion on his feet to keep time with the juggling.
* **Eyes:** Darting wildly left, right, and up to track the flying packets.
* **Effects:** The data packets leave tiny, fading motion trails as they arc through the air.

## 8. The Sweeper (Garbage Collection / Clearing Cache)
* **Action:** Clawd holds a tiny pixel-art push broom and is vigorously sweeping the ground.
* **Body Mechanics:** Leaning forward, stepping into the sweep, and pulling back in a repetitive, rhythmic cycle.
* **Eyes:** Focused downward with a look of pure determination.
* **Effects:** Little pixel "dust bunnies" and broken data fragments tumbling away in front of the broom.

## 9. The Wizard (Refactoring / "Magic" Optimization)
* **Action:** Clawd is wearing a tiny, crooked wizard hat and dramatically waving a wand.
* **Body Mechanics:** Majestic, swooping motions with his arms, bobbing up and down.
* **Eyes:** Closed in deep concentration, then bursting open with a bright, happy blink when the spell "casts".
* **Effects:** A swirl of multicolored pixel stars rising up and spinning around him in a magical vortex.

## 10. The Overheated (High CPU / Memory Warning)
* **Action:** Clawd is sitting down (splooted), completely exhausted, fanning himself with one hand.
* **Body Mechanics:** Heavy, labored, asymmetrical breathing (a very exaggerated, slow squash and stretch).
* **Eyes:** Half-closed and looking very tired.
* **Effects:** Little pixel puffs of steam or smoke rising off the top of his head.
